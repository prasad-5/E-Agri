Precautions to be taken in the use of insecticides:
  • 1.The toxic effect of the pesticide should be known by the color of the triangle on the cans.
    • 90%

    Red - highly toxic

    70%

    Turmeric - more toxic

    50%

    Blue - general toxicity

    30%

    Green - less toxic

  • 2.Read labels and leaflets before use. A face mask should be worn while spraying.
  • 3.Store and lock away out of reach of children and pets.
  • 4.Do not take the lid of the pesticide container in the mouth. Check that the pesticide container and packet are correct and take out the bill..
  • 5.Close the lids of medicine cans with small tools .
  • 6. Hands should be sprayed with rubber gloves, ears should be covered with cotton, nose mask should be worn and face protection should be worn. A hat should be worn on the head.
  • 7.The whole body should not be covered, shoes should be worn on the feet.
  • 8.To collect the pesticide, use a dropper glass. Mix the required amount.
  • 9.Mix the medicine solution with sour cream. Do not mix with hands.
  • 10.Before cleaning the vazile, gently wipe the pee without blowing it with your mouth.
  • 11.Spray early in the airing stage.
  • 12. Use a good quality non-perforated spray. Wash contaminated clothing and body with water. Do not spray by children.
  • 13.The plants should be sprayed so that they are completely wet.
  • 14.Spray during cold weather.
  • 15.Do not eat or drink while spraying.
  • 16.Drinks and food should not be taken while spraying.
  • 17.After spraying, wash your hands thoroughly before eating food.
  • 18.Clothes should be cleaned for nutrients in comparison to pesticides.
  • 19.Clothes should be cleaned after spraying.
  • 20. Break up used cans and bury them in the ground.
  • 21.Take a bath after spraying the medicine in the evening.
  • 22. A warning board has been put up for those who have not taken care to prevent the environment from being polluted.
  • 23.If there is a toxic effect due to drug ingestion, first aid should be given, then immediately consult a doctor and get medical treatment.
  • 24.Pesticides should not be transported along with foodstuffs.
