1. Sri Rice Cultivation Method

2. The differences between the general method and the Sri method.

topic general method Sri Method
seed An acre requires 50 to 60 kg of seed. Just 2 kg of seed per acre is sufficient.
Method of sowing Seedlings that are 30 days old are used. Seedlings that are 8 to 12 days old (two-day stage) are used.
Number of plants present in each cluster Generally, 3 to 4 plants are planted very deep in each cluster. The same sapling is planted very carefully in each cluster.
Use of fertilizers/pesticides Chemical fertilizers, pesticides and other weeds need to be used. In this method, organic fertilizers are preferred. Generally pesticides and other pesticides are not required and traditional organic medicines can be used.
Water management There is always a need to store water It is enough to make the field wet
Weed management Weeds are removed with labourers and put on the embankment. Some of the weeds are used if the threat of weeds is high Weeds are used as a green rota when weeds are mixed in the soil by using a device called weeder.
